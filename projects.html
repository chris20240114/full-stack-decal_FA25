Projects

Making Music with Ableton

I write short electronic and lo-fi tracks in Ableton Live. I sketch ideas with a MIDI keyboard, then arrange, automate, and mix with EQ, compression, and reverb. I try to keep each idea small and repeatable so I can iterate quickly.

  • Tools: Ableton Live, MIDI keyboard, stock devices/plugins
  • Techniques: sidechain compression, subtractive EQ, sends/returns, automation lanes
  • Outcome: I survived IB music (yay); practice with mixing and gain staging

High-School Marketplace Website

A simple buy/sell site I built in high school to learn full-stack basics. Users could browse listings, add items to a cart, and complete a mock checkout (no real payments). I focused on clear UI and clean separation of data from presentation.

  • Stack: HTML/CSS/JS on the front end; Django/sqlite for back end
  • Features: product cards, search/filter, inboxes, cart, order summary and etc...
  • How it ended: Insufficient fund to host it yearround... but I learned a lot!
